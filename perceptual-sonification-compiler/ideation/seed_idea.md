Idea: A DSL and optimizing compiler for data sonification that compiles declarative data-to-sound mapping specifications into bounded-latency audio renderers, using psychoacoustic cost models (critical-band masking, JND thresholds, auditory-stream-segregation constraints) and information-theoretic objectives to automatically select and tune mapping parameters for maximum perceptual discriminability under cognitive-load budgets, with compositional semantics enabling modular multi-stream sonification design.
